This website helps people make tough decisions by asking an AI for advice. It gives you pros and cons and questions to think about.

I made this because sometimes it's hard to decide things and it would be cool if AI could help. The AI looks at your problem and gives you different ways to think about it.

How It Works

1. Share Your Decision

Describe the choice you're facing and provide relevant context. The more details you share, the better guidance we can provide.

2. AI Analysis

Our AI analyzes your situation and generates structured guidance including pros, cons, and important considerations.

3. Gain Insights

Receive balanced perspectives, overlooked factors, and reflective questions to help clarify your thinking.

4. Make Your Choice

Use the insights to make an informed decision that aligns with your values and circumstances.

Humanitarian Impact

Breaking Down Barriers: Decision-making guidance has traditionally been available only to those who can afford professional counselors, coaches, or mentors. Our platform democratizes access to thoughtful analysis, ensuring that anyone with internet access can receive structured guidance.
